`useState` is considered to be the most basic of all the hooks provided by React.
It is also the one you are most likely to use, alongside `useEffect`.

Yet over the last couple of months, I have seen this hook being misused a lot.
This has mostly nothing todo with the hook itself, but because state management is never easy.

This is the first part of a series I'm calling useState pitfalls.
